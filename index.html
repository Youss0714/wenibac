About Us

Recognized expertise in transit and transport

Our History

Founded by Mr. Yacubu Dawuda, Wenibac Advanced Shipping Ltd was born from a clear vision: to facilitate trade in Africa and offer businesses a reliable, fast and transparent transit partner. With over 30 years of experience in transit, import-export and transport, we have established ourselves as a reference at the Port of Tema.

Our Mission

To facilitate trade by offering innovative, reliable and personalized transit solutions. We are committed to being our clients' trusted partner in their commercial development.
